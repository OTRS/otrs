What is a typical scenario for using OpenTRS? Example: Bob is a manufactor of VCRs and his customers often have problems to program the VCRs. So they send Bob an e-\/mail. Sometimes they send a second e-\/mail to show Bob how important their request is. They are wondering if Bob is alive and how fast he will answer. Bob is using a normal INBOX and reads his e-\/mails with pine, mutt or what ever e-\/mail client. Sometimes his two brothers Tim and Joe help him to answer the e-\/mails. They all use the same INBOX. Of course they have no clue that one customer wrote two e-\/mails and maybe Tim give a different answer to the first e-\/mail for the client that his brother Joe does.
